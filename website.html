All about mental health

Whole health begins with mental health

Perhaps you're feeling down or anxious, struggling with substance use, or sensing that you aren't living the life you were meant to lead. Whatever your needs may be, we're here to offer support — and we understand that reaching out can be the hardest step.

At Polaris Mental Health Services, we adopt a patient-first philosophy. We aim to exceed expectations, one individual and one community at a time, delivering comprehensive psychiatric care in a private, compassionate, and friendly atmosphere.

Current medications

Please bring — or have a way to access — all prescribed medications at your first appointment. Your provider will prescribe enough medication to last until your next scheduled medication management appointment. If you miss a scheduled appointment, refills are at the provider's discretion, so please keep your scheduled appointments.

Cancellations & no-show policy

We require a minimum of 48 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment and avoid a fee. Appointments missed, cancelled, or rescheduled with less than 48 hours' notice incur a $75 fee, which is not covered by insurance and will be charged to the credit card on file.

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, your appointment will be cancelled and the cancellation fee assessed.

Treatment dismissal policy

You have the right to end care at any time and for any reason. We reserve the right to end treatment if:

  • We suspect prescription medication abuse or undisclosed substance use
  • A prescription drug contract is violated during treatment
  • We believe our services are no longer beneficial to you
  • We believe you would be better served by a higher level of care or a different provider
  • There is a pattern of frequent cancellations, rescheduling, or no-shows
  • Interpersonal dynamics are not conducive to a therapeutic relationship
  • You or a family member are hostile or aggressive toward providers, staff, or other clients
Emergencies & medication refills

In a psychiatric emergency, go to your local Emergency Department, call 911, or call/text 988.

Medications are not refilled outside of business hours, including holidays, nights, and weekends. Please allow 72 hours (excluding weekends and holidays) for refill requests, and request refills in a timely manner. We reserve the right to decline refills at any time.
